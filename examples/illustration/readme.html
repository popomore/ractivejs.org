You can take illustrations produced with Inkscape or Adobe Illustrator and add interactivity to them by hand-editing the SVG source code.

It's not exactly straightforward – you have to know your way around SVG, and you have to deal with all the cruft that illustration software adds to the markup – but it's possible, and Ractive.js makes it easier than it otherwise would be. Hopefully, in future, we'll have better tools for allowing artists and developers to work together on the web.
